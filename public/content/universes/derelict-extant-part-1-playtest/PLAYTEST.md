# Playtest Feedback

## What Worked

- Curious opening-room pacing is appropriate.

## Bugs or Confusion

- Passive observations were presented as too many separate actions.
- One-use actions displayed redundant 1/1 counts.
- Suit Air changed only when actions completed.
- Home chat became too small when several actions were visible.
- Air canisters could not be used from Inventory.
- Tracing the captain's route left no next action or stated motivation.

## Desired Edits

- Use one orientation action followed by up to three short narrative beats.
- Reserve buttons for concrete choices and explicitly state when a room is
  exhausted.
- Consume Suit Air continuously and tune its rate from measured route duration.
- Keep action and chat panels equal height with independent scrolling.
- Give carried items globally usable Inventory actions.
- Keep every revealed route playable and state the character's immediate goal.

The first run spent about 53 acting seconds between pressurizing the suit and
the newly completed captain's-quarters arrival route. At -105/min, that path
uses 92.75 air and arrives with 7.25 before optional canister use.
